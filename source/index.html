Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers

Do you work with businesses outside of the Rio Grande Valley?

Yes. We are based in Harlingen, TX, and we work with clients remotely using secure access and clear monthly workflows.

What does monthly bookkeeping typically include?

Reconciliations, clean categorization, review for accuracy, and monthly reporting. The exact scope depends on your systems and transaction volume.

Can you help if my books are months behind?

Yes. Cleanup and catch-up work is common. The first step is the Financial Health Check so we can estimate the timeline and identify the biggest issues.

Do you file business taxes?

We focus on bookkeeping and getting your books tax-ready. We can coordinate with your CPA or connect you with a tax professional if you need one.

How do you price your services?

Pricing depends on the current state of the books, transaction volume, number of accounts, and how much support you need. We give clear scope and pricing after the initial review.
